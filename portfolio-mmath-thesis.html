Optimal Sensor Location Research

  • August, 2015

  • Thesis

  • American Control Conference

For my master's thesis work at University of Waterloo, I worked on the optimal sensor location problem in the Infinite Dimensional Systems research group. My work specifically focused on finding the most optimal discretization technique to model the relevant infinite dimensional systems. The work was presented at the American Control Conference in 2015.
